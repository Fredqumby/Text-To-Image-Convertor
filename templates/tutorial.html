How to Use Image to Text Converter

Follow these simple steps to extract text from your images

1

Upload Your Image

Start by uploading an image file containing the text you want to extract.

How to upload:
  • Click on the upload area or drag and drop your image
  • Supported formats: PNG, JPG, JPEG, GIF, BMP, TIFF, WEBP
  • No file size limit - process any image instantly
  • Your image will appear as a preview once uploaded
2

Convert to Text

Click the "Convert to Text" button to start the OCR process.

What happens:
  • AI-powered OCR scans your image for text
  • Processing typically takes 5-15 seconds
  • Multiple OCR engines ensure best accuracy
  • Automatic retry if first attempt fails
3

Copy or Download

Once text is extracted, you can copy it to clipboard or download as a file.

Your options:
  • Copy: One-click copy to clipboard for easy pasting
  • Download: Save as .txt file to your device
  • Edit: Text appears in editable format
  • Reset: Clear everything to start over

Pro Tips for Best Results

  • Use high-resolution, clear images for better accuracy
  • Ensure good contrast between text and background
  • Avoid blurry, rotated, or heavily stylized fonts
  • Screenshots of documents work excellently
  • Multiple languages are supported automatically
  • No registration required - completely free to use
