How We Use Your Information

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google uses cookies (including the DART cookie) to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

For more information about Google's advertising policies, visit: Google Ad Policies.

OCR.space API

We use the OCR.space API to process your uploaded images. Your images are sent to OCR.space for text extraction and are not stored by us. Please review OCR.space's privacy policy at ocr.space for more information about their data handling practices.
